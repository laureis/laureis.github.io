indie folk influenced swedish band based in Berlin

"From Sweden comes Tula, which means something like 'to wander far into the forest' and its basically a female singer from the folk tradition. Very sparse on the music side, with just a bit of electric piano, some guitar, a slow bang on the reduced drum kit and Tula's voice on top, sometimes double tracked. Dream pop, absolutely folk like. An excellent breezy of great elegant singing and folky music."

- VITAL WEEKLEY
